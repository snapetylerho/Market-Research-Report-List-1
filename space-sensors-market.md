<p><p>The Space Sensors market is categorized into three main types: Surface Sensors, Immersion Sensors, and Others. Surface Sensors are designed to monitor environmental conditions from a fixed position on spacecraft or satellites, measuring parameters like temperature and radiation. Immersion Sensors are used in fluids, gathering data from within a liquid environment, often essential for spacecraft operating in various atmospheric conditions. The "Others" category encompasses specialized sensors tailored for unique applications, integrating technologies such as fiber optics and advanced imaging systems.</p></p>
