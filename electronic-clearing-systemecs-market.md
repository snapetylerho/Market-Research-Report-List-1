<p><p>The Electronic Clearing System (ECS) is a method used for transferring funds electronically between banks, facilitating bulk payments and collections. ECS can be categorized into two market types: ECS Credit and ECS Debit. ECS Credit allows for bulk crediting of funds, enabling businesses to disburse salaries, dividends, or payments to multiple recipients in a single transaction. Conversely, ECS Debit permits the automatic deduction of funds from a customer's account for recurring payments like utility bills or loan installments, streamlining payment processes efficiently.</p></p>
