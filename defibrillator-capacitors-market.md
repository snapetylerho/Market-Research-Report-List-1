<p><strong>The Defibrillator Capacitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monophasic Defibrillator Capacitor</li><li>Biphasic Defibrillator Capacitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The defibrillator capacitors market is primarily categorized into monophasic and biphasic types. Monophasic defibrillator capacitors discharge a single electrical shock in one direction, traditionally used in older devices. In contrast, biphasic defibrillator capacitors deliver a shock that reverses polarity, improving efficacy and reducing myocardial damage. The biphasic design is increasingly preferred for its effectiveness in treating arrhythmias and enhancing patient outcomes, leading to its growing adoption in modern defibrillation technology.</p></p>
