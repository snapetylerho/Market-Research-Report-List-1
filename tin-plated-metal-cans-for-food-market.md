<p><p>Tin-plated metal cans for food are primarily categorized into two types: three-piece cans and two-piece cans. Three-piece cans consist of a cylindrical body, top, and bottom, typically welded together, making them suitable for products like vegetables and soups. In contrast, two-piece cans are formed from a single sheet of metal, creating a body and a lid, offering a seamless design that reduces potential leaks and enhances strength, making them ideal for beverages and perishable goods.</p></p>
