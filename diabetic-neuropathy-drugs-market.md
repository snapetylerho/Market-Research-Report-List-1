<p><p>The diabetic neuropathy drugs market includes several types of treatments, primarily focusing on pain relief and symptom management. Calcium channel alpha-2 delta ligands, such as gabapentin and pregabalin, reduce nerve pain by modulating calcium channels. SNRIs (serotonin-norepinephrine reuptake inhibitors) like duloxetine enhance serotonin and norepinephrine levels, alleviating neuropathic pain. Tricyclic antidepressants (TCAs) provide analgesic effects through neurotransmitter modulation. Other treatments may include opioid analgesics, topical agents, and various experimental therapies, catering to diverse patient needs in managing diabetic neuropathy.</p></p>
