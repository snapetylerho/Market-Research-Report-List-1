<p><p>The sleep apnea treatment devices market includes various types aimed at improving airflow during sleep. Positive Airway Pressure (PAP) devices, such as CPAP, maintain open airways using continuous airflow. Oxygen devices supplement oxygen to enhance breathing quality. Oral appliances are custom-fitted dental devices that reposition the jaw to keep airways clear. Adaptive Servo Ventilation (ASV) devices provide varying pressure levels based on detected breathing patterns, effectively treating complex sleep apnea. Each type offers unique solutions to improve patient comfort and sleep quality.</p></p>
