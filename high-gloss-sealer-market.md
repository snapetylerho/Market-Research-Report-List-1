<p><strong>The High Gloss Sealer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Film-forming Sealers</li><li>Penetrating Sealers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high gloss sealer market primarily features two types: film-forming sealers and penetrating sealers. Film-forming sealers create a glossy protective layer on surfaces, enhancing appearance and providing durability against stains and wear. They are commonly used on concrete, wood, and masonry. In contrast, penetrating sealers infiltrate the substrate, offering protection from within while preserving the natural look of the material. They are ideal for porous surfaces, safeguarding against moisture and contaminants without altering the surface finish.</p></p>
