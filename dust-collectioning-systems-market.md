Cartridge dust collectors utilize pleated filters for high filtration efficiency and compact design. Electrostatic precipitators employ electrical charges to capture and remove particulate matter from exhaust streams. Other types may include baghouse collectors and cyclones, catering to diverse industrial needs and contributing to improved environmental compliance and workplace safety.</p></p>
