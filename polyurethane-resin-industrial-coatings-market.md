<p><p>The polyurethane resin industrial coatings market is divided into two primary types: solvent-based coatings and waterborne coatings. Solvent-based coatings use organic solvents to dissolve the resin, offering excellent durability and chemical resistance, making them suitable for various industrial applications. In contrast, waterborne coatings utilize water as a primary solvent, providing lower environmental impact and reduced volatile organic compound emissions. They are increasingly popular due to regulatory requirements and a growing emphasis on sustainability, while still delivering good performance and flexibility.</p></p>
