<p><p>The energy-saving centrifugal fan market is categorized into three main types based on blade design: forward, backward, and radial fans. Forward fans have curved blades that allow for high airflow at low pressure, making them efficient for ventilation. Backward fans feature curved blades that provide higher efficiency and pressure, suitable for systems requiring strong airflow against resistance. Radial fans offer a straight blade configuration, delivering high-pressure airflow, ideal for applications needing powerful ventilation. Each type serves distinct operational needs in various industries.</p></p>
