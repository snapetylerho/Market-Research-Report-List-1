<p><p>The sampling oscilloscope market is primarily divided into digital and analog oscilloscopes. Digital oscilloscopes convert analog signals into digital form for processing, allowing for advanced features like storage, analysis, and complex triggering. They are favored for their accuracy and versatility in modern applications. Analog oscilloscopes, on the other hand, display signals in real-time on a screen using continuous waveforms, making them simpler and often preferred for educational purposes and basic diagnostics. Each type serves specific needs in electronic testing and measurement.</p></p>
