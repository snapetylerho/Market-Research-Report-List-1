<p><p>The clamp meter market features three primary types: Current Transformer Clamp Meters, which utilize coils to measure alternating current (AC) by transforming the current into a proportional output; Hall Effect Clamp Meters, which leverage the Hall effect to measure both AC and direct current (DC) accurately; and Flexible Clamp Meters, designed with flexible sensors to measure current in tight or awkward spaces. Each type caters to specific measurement needs, enhancing versatility in electrical diagnostics and monitoring applications.</p></p>
