<p><ul><li>Spherical Power</li><li>Cylindrical Power</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Portable Automatic Kerato Refractometer market is categorized into two main types: Spherical Power and Cylindrical Power. Spherical Power measures the curvature of the cornea to determine refractive errors such as myopia or hyperopia, essential for prescribing corrective lenses. Cylindrical Power, on the other hand, assesses astigmatism by evaluating the cornea's irregularities. Both types of measurements are crucial for eye care professionals, enhancing diagnosis and treatment in various settings, including clinics and outreach programs.</p></p>
