<p><strong>The Consumer Grade 3D Printers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FDM Technology</li><li>SLA Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The consumer-grade 3D printer market primarily features two technologies: FDM (Fused Deposition Modeling) and SLA (Stereolithography). FDM printers use thermoplastic filaments that are melted and extruded to build objects layer by layer, making them popular for affordability and ease of use. In contrast, SLA printers employ ultraviolet light to cure liquid resins, producing high-resolution prints with intricate details. While FDM is favored for prototyping and DIY projects, SLA is preferred for applications requiring precision, such as jewelry and dental models.</p></p>
