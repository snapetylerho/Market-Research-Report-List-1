<p><p>The automotive thermal management valve market includes two key types: the intake throttle valve and the exhaust throttle valve. The intake throttle valve regulates airflow into the engine, enhancing fuel efficiency and optimizing performance by controlling air volume and maintaining optimal operating temperatures. Conversely, the exhaust throttle valve manages exhaust flow, improving emission control and engine efficiency by adjusting back pressure and thermal regulation. Both valves are crucial for managing engine temperatures and ensuring optimal performance and compliance with environmental standards.</p></p>
