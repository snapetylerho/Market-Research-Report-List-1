<p><p>Automated Transit Networks (ATN) encompass various systems designed for efficient urban transportation. Personal Rapid Transit (PRT) focuses on small, automated vehicles that transport individual passengers or small groups on guided paths, offering direct, on-demand service. In contrast, Group Rapid Transit (GRT) accommodates larger groups, utilizing automated vehicles that operate on fixed routes with scheduled stops, improving capacity and efficiency for high-density areas. Both systems aim to enhance urban mobility while reducing congestion and environmental impact.</p></p>
