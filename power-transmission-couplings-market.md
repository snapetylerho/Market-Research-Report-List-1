Elastomeric element couplings use elastic materials to absorb shock and vibration, enhancing system durability and performance. Metallic membrane or disc type couplings provide high torque transmission with low backlash, ideal for precision applications. Together, these coupling types cater to various industrial needs, ensuring reliable and efficient power transfer across different machinery.</p></p>
