<p><p>Non-contact laser distance sensors are devices that measure the distance to a target without physical contact, utilizing the time-of-flight principle or phase shift method. These sensors emit a laser beam that reflects off the target and calculates distance based on the return time of the light. They are widely used in various industries, including construction, automotive, and robotics, for applications like surveying, level measurement, and object detection.</p><p>The Non-contact Laser Distance Sensor Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.80% during the forecast period.
