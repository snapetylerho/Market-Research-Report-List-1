<p><p>The TPU car paint protection film market is segmented into aliphatic and aromatic TPU types. Aliphatic TPU films are known for their superior clarity, UV resistance, and durability, making them ideal for long-term protection against environmental damage. In contrast, aromatic TPU films offer cost-effective solutions with good mechanical properties but less UV stability, resulting in potential yellowing over time. Each type serves distinct customer needs, balancing durability, aesthetic appeal, and budget considerations in automotive applications.</p></p>
