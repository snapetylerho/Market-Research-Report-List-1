<p><p>The Dual and Multi Energy Computed Tomography (CT) market encompasses diverse technologies for enhanced imaging capabilities. Dual-source CT uses two X-ray tubes to simultaneously capture images, improving speed and reducing motion artifacts. Rapid kVp switching enables quick alternation between different energy levels during a single scan, enhancing material differentiation and improving tissue characterization. Both techniques optimize image clarity, support advanced diagnostic applications, and aid in precise treatment planning, driving their adoption in hospitals and clinics for improved patient outcomes.</p></p>
