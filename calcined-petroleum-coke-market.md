<p><strong>The Calcined Petroleum Coke Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Needle Coke</li><li>Shot Coke</li><li>Sponge Coke</li><li>Honeycomb Coke</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The calcined petroleum coke market consists of several types including needle coke, shot coke, sponge coke, and honeycomb coke. Needle coke, known for its high purity and excellent electrical conductivity, is primarily used in the production of electrodes. Shot coke, characterized by its spherical shape, is typically utilized in aluminum production. Sponge coke has a porous structure, making it suitable for various industrial applications. Honeycomb coke, with its unique honeycomb-like structure, is often used in specialty applications requiring specific physical properties.</p></p>
