<p><ul><li>Press Molding</li><li>Casting Molding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The kitchen benchtop market features various manufacturing techniques, notably press molding and casting molding. Press molding involves shaping materials under high pressure to create durable, uniform surfaces, ideal for contemporary designs. In contrast, casting molding uses liquid materials poured into molds, solidifying to form intricate shapes; this method allows for greater customization and complexity. Both techniques cater to different aesthetic and functional needs in kitchen designs, driving innovation and variety within the market.</p></p>
