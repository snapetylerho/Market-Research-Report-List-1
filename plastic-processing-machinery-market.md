<p><p>The plastic processing machinery market encompasses various types of equipment essential for shaping and manufacturing plastic products. Injection Molding Machines (IMM) are used for producing intricate parts by injecting molten plastic into molds. Extrusion machines process plastic into continuous shapes, such as films or pipes, by forcing material through a die. Blow Molding Machines (BMM) create hollow plastic products by inflating a heated plastic tube within a mold. Collectively, these machines play a crucial role in the efficiency and versatility of plastic production.</p></p>
