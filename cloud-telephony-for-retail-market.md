<p><p>Cloud Telephony for the retail market encompasses two primary types: Unlimited Cloud Telephony and Metered Cloud Telephony. Unlimited Cloud Telephony offers businesses a flat-rate plan, allowing for unrestricted call volume and lower costs for high-call operations, enhancing customer interaction. Conversely, Metered Cloud Telephony charges based on usage, making it ideal for smaller retailers or those with fluctuating call volumes who prefer to pay only for what they use, providing flexibility and cost efficiency in communication strategies.</p></p>
