<p><ul><li>Syringeless Power Injectors</li><li>Dual-syringe Power Injectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CT contrast injectors market is categorized into two main types: syringeless power injectors and dual-syringe power injectors. Syringeless power injectors streamline the injection process by using a closed system to minimize contamination and reduce the need for manual handling of syringes. Dual-syringe power injectors feature two syringes to enable simultaneous contrast and saline administration, enhancing imaging quality and patient comfort. Both types improve the efficiency and effectiveness of contrast delivery during CT scans, catering to varying clinical needs.</p></p>
