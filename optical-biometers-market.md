<p><strong>The Optical Biometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Type</li><li>Non-contact Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical biometer market is segmented into two main types: contact and non-contact. </p><p>Contact optical biometers require direct contact with the patient's eye to measure parameters like axial length and corneal curvature, offering high accuracy but potentially causing discomfort. </p><p>In contrast, non-contact optical biometers utilize focused light beams to gather measurements without eye contact, enhancing patient comfort and minimizing the risk of infection. This distinction caters to diverse clinical needs and patient preferences, driving innovation and adoption in ophthalmic practices.</p></p>
