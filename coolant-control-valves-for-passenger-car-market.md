<p><p>Coolant control valves regulate the flow of coolant in a passenger car's engine system. </p><p>- **2-Way Valves** allow flow in one direction, controlling coolant to and from components like radiators.</p><p>- **3-Way Valves** manage flow between two paths, directing coolant to various engine areas or bypassing when necessary.</p><p>- **4-Way Valves** enable coolant to switch between multiple paths, often used in advanced temperature regulation systems.</p><p>- **5-Way Valves** provide even more routing options for precise control.</p><p>- **Others** encompass specialized designs or configurations tailored for specific vehicle requirements.</p></p>
