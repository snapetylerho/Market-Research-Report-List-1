Smooth PP films offer better electrical performance and are preferred for high-frequency applications, providing uniform dielectric properties. In contrast, rough PP films provide enhanced surface area, improving capacitance and energy storage capabilities, making them suitable for energy-intensive applications. Both types cater to distinct market needs, with smooth films focusing on precision and efficiency, while rough films emphasize energy capacity and reliability.</p></p>
