<p><p>The Elevator Overspeed Governor market is mainly segmented into two types: electronic and mechanical. Electronic governors use advanced sensors and microcontrollers to monitor the elevator's speed and ensure safety by activating brakes if overspeed is detected. They offer precise control and faster response times. In contrast, mechanical governors rely on centrifugal force and mechanical components to sense speed, providing a more traditional approach. Both types aim to prevent accidents and enhance elevator safety, appealing to different customer preferences and installation needs.</p></p>
