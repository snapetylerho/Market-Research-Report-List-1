<p><p>The Bone Void Filling Container market consists of two main types: single tube and double tube systems. In contrast, double tube systems allow for the separate delivery of two components, such as a bone graft and a mediating agent, ensuring optimal mixing at the time of application. Each type serves distinct applications in orthopedics, catering to diverse surgical needs and preferences.</p></p>
