<p><p>The mechanical torque meter market features various types, including the spring type and flexible rod type. Spring type torque meters utilize a calibrated spring mechanism to measure torque by observing the deformation of the spring under load. In contrast, flexible rod torque meters employ a rod that bends under torque, allowing for precise measurement based on the degree of bending. Both types are crucial for applications in manufacturing, automotive, and engineering, providing reliable torque measurements for quality control and performance testing.</p></p>
