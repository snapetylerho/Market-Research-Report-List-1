<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Roofing Underlayment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Bitumen Synthetic</li><li>Asphalt-Saturated Felt</li><li>Rubberized Asphalt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Roofing underlayment serves as a protective barrier beneath roofing materials. The market includes three primary types: </p><p>1. **Non-Bitumen Synthetic** underlayment is lightweight and highly water-resistant, providing excellent durability against harsh weather. </p><p>2. **Asphalt-Saturated Felt** is a tried-and-true material made from organic or fiberglass felt soaked in asphalt, offering good protection but is heavier and less water-resistant than synthetic options. </p><p>3. **Rubberized Asphalt** combines asphalt with polymers, delivering superior water resistance and flexibility, making it suitable for more challenging roofing applications.</p></p>
