<p><p>The mammography screening market consists primarily of two types: molybdenum target X-ray systems and ultrasonic inspection tools. Molybdenum target X-ray systems use X-rays to create detailed images of breast tissue, aiding in early cancer detection through high-resolution imaging. In contrast, ultrasonic inspection employs sound waves to evaluate breast tissue, often complementing X-ray results. Both methods are vital for effective screening, allowing for early diagnosis and improved patient outcomes in breast health management.</p></p>
