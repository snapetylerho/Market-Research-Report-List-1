<p><p>The automotive knuckle arm market is categorized into three main types based on material: ductile iron, white cast iron, and grey cast iron. Ductile iron type knuckles are known for their high strength and ductility, making them suitable for demanding applications. White cast iron type knuckles offer excellent wear resistance but are brittle, limiting their use. Grey cast iron type knuckles, characterized by good machinability and vibration damping, are widely used due to their cost-effectiveness and reliable performance in various vehicles.</p></p>
