<p><ul><li>Dynamic</li><li>Condenser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The professional microphone market is primarily divided into dynamic and condenser types. Dynamic microphones, known for their durability and ability to handle high sound pressure levels, are ideal for live performances and recording loud sound sources. In contrast, condenser microphones are sensitive and offer a wider frequency response, making them suitable for studio recording and capturing nuanced vocals and instruments. Each type serves distinct applications, catering to the varying needs of musicians, sound engineers, and content creators.</p></p>
