<p><p>Board mount humidity sensors are crucial for various applications, categorized mainly into analog and digital output types. Analog output sensors provide continuous voltage or current signals proportional to humidity levels, facilitating straightforward integration into systems. In contrast, digital output sensors deliver humidity data through a digital signal, enhancing precision and enabling easier communication with microcontrollers. Both types are designed for compact installations on circuit boards, catering to industries like HVAC, consumer electronics, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
