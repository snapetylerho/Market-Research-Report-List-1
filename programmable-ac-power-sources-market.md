<p><p>The Programmable AC Power Sources market includes two primary types: Series Mode and Parallel Mode. Series Mode operates by providing power where output voltage is adjustable while maintaining a constant current, ideal for testing high-voltage equipment. Parallel Mode focuses on delivering higher current outputs by combining multiple units, making it suitable for low-voltage, high-current applications. Both modes cater to various sectors, including electronics testing, renewable energy simulations, and aerospace, enhancing flexibility and reliability in power supply solutions.</p></p>
