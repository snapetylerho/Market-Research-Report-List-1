<p><p>The stand-alone nebulizers market includes three primary types: pneumatic, ultrasonic, and mesh nebulizers. Pneumatic nebulizers utilize compressed air to convert liquid medication into mist, making them widely used for their effectiveness and affordability. Ultrasonic nebulizers use high-frequency vibrations to generate aerosol, offering quieter operation and faster treatment times. Mesh nebulizers employ a mesh diaphragm to produce fine aerosol particles, ensuring efficient drug delivery and portability. Each type caters to different patient needs and preferences in respiratory therapy.</p></p>
